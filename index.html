How to Play

2-6 players journey from Elementary School through Graduation (Age 18).

Spin the wheel to move forward along the path.

Challenge tiles (blue): Answer the question yourself for a perfect score, or spin for a random grade! Below 6 = fail, 10 = ace it!

Chance tiles (yellow): Draw a random card — could be good or bad!

Choice tiles (purple): Pick your path. Bad past choices = fewer options!

Career tiles (orange): Career guidance moments that shape your future.

At graduation: Your life is projected to retirement age. Good choices = wealth & happiness. Bad choices = struggle.

Every decision matters! Make good choices early for the best outcomes later in life.
